Disintegration is the strongest offensive spell among the holy magic. Disintegration is an attack that causes spiritrons to fall apart and disperse, erasing everything, both physical and spiritual.

## Information

| Type | Unique Skill |
|------|--------------|
| Obtain Cost | 100000 MP |
| Points to Master | 1000 |

---

## Obtaining

Disintegration is a Unique Skill that can be obtained through:

- Reincarnation
- Skill Reroll

---

## Usage

[Active - Hold] Disintegration - The user channels energy into a focused attack. While holding the skill, a disintegration field is formed at the target location or aimed entity.

- Hold Duration: 10 seconds
- Damage: 1500
- On release after full charge, a Disintegration Entity is deployed at the target location or entity position
- If released early, the skill is cancelled and the attack fails

---

## Notes

- This skill is **not intended for standard player use**
- It is not fully balanced for normal gameplay
- Designed primarily as a fun to use Unique Skill
