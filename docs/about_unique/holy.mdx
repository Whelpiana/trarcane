A divine blessing that shifts the user's soul alignment to Holy. This skill purges the wicked by infusing strikes with holy power and grants ultimate authority over Holy Magic.

## Information

| Type | Unique Skill |
|------|--------------|
| Obtain Cost | 100000 Magicules |
| Points to Master | 1000 |
| Passive | In Slot / True |

---

## Obtaining

Holy is a Unique Skill that can be obtained through:

- Reincarnation
- Skill Reroll

---

## Usage

### [Passive / Toggle] Divine Awakening
Upon learning the skill or undergoing a race evolution, the user undergoes an alignment transformation:
- Forces user alignment to Holy (overriding Majin, Chaos, or Default states).
- Grants the user **Blessed** status.
- Automatically learning the Extra Skill: Heavenly Eye.

---

### [In-Slot] Holy Blessing
Enhances the user's offensive combat:
- Physical Attacks: Infuses non-holy physical strikes with an additional +50.0 Holy Damage.

---

### [Passive] Hero's Awakening
Continuously monitors the user's status profile:
- If the user achieves the status of a True Hero, they are automatically granted the Extra Skill: Law Manipulation.

---

### [Active - Held] Disintegration
Holding down the skill key initiates the chant sequence.

- Hold Duration: 215 Ticks (~10.75 seconds)
- Cost: 200,000 Magicules
- Damage: - Fully completing the channel inflicts 300 Disintegration Damage per second for 3 seconds.
- Cooldown: 15 minutes on completion or cancellation.

---

## Notes

- If the user's magicule count drop below the tick requirements during chanting, the spell cancels itself.
- Will persistently reset user Alignment properties to holy thresholds even across evolutions.
