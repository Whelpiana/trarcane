It greatly enhances the user's eyesight and allows them to see through anything; nothing can hide from their dragon eyes.

## Information

| Type | Unique Skill |
|------|--------------|
| Obtain Cost | 30000 MP |
| Points to Master | 1000 |
| Passive | Toggle |

---

## Obtaining

Milim Eye is a Unique Skill that can be obtained through:

- Divine Dragon Race while having the Wrath Unique Skill
- Skill Reroll or Reincarnation (if added to the configs)

---

## Usage

[Passive / Toggle] Presence Sense - Enhances the user's perception.

- Grants Presence Sense (6.0) while active
---

[Active - Press] Analytical Appraisal - Enables advanced analysis of entities and blocks.

- Analysis Level: 1 (4 when mastered)
- Analysis Range: Self-targeted (up to 12 blocks via targeting system)

---

## Bonus

Upon acquiring Milim Eye makes Mana Manipulation learnable for the user.
