It that enhances the user's vision tremendously.

## Information

| Type | Extra Skill |
|------|-------------|
| Obtain Cost | 1000 MP |
| Points to Master | 500 |
| Passive | Toggle |

---

## Obtaining

Celestial Eye is an Extra Skill that can be obtained through:

- Being Divine Bird
- Predation

---

## Usage

[Passive - Toggle] - Highlights all mobs/players in a 30x30 area in blue, even ones under disguising skills. Presence Sense (5) is granted while active.
