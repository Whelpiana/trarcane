## Information

| Type | Extra Skill |
|------|-------------|

## Obtaining

Rest is an Extra Skill that can be obtained through:

- Reaching 200,000 EP or higher.

---

## Usage

### [Active - Press] Aura Restoration - Instantly restores the user's Aura.

- Cooldown: 30 minutes.
