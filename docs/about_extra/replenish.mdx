Instantly restores your Magicules to maximum capacity.

## Information

| Type | Extra Skill |
|------|-------------|
| Obtain Cost | 1000 MP |

---

## Obtaining

Replenish is an Extra Skill that can be obtained through:

- Reaching 400,000 EP or higher.

---

## Usage

### [Active - Press] Magicule Restoration - Instantly restores the user's Magicules.

- Cooldown: 30 minutes.
